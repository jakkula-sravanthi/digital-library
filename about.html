About Digital Library
Digital Library is a virtual repository of learning resources which is not just a
repository with search/browse facilities but provides a host of services for the learner community. It is
sponsored and mentored by Ministry of Education, Government of India, through its National Mission on
Education through Information and Communication Technology (NMEICT).
